FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid driving license, government ID proof (Aadhaar/Passport), and a credit/debit card for the security deposit.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car is 21 years. You must hold a valid driving license for at least 1 year.

Is insurance included in the rental?

Yes, all our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage. Additional coverage options are available at an extra cost.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the pickup time. Cancellation charges may apply.

What happens if I return the car late?

Late returns are charged on an hourly basis. Please contact us in advance if you need to extend your rental period.
